Our Mission

At Sissies House, we are dedicated to supporting victims of domestic violence while recognizing the importance of rehabilitation for perpetrators. We believe that, although the relationship may be irreparably damaged, helping those who have caused harm is essential to breaking the cycle of violence. Our innovative rehabilitation programs focus on accountability and meaningful change, collaborating with local law enforcement to ensure a safer community for all. By providing tailored support and resources, we strive to empower individuals to heal and transform their lives, working together toward a future free from violence.

Vision Statement

We envision a community where every individual lives free from the threat of domestic violence. By fostering a culture of accountability and healing, we aim to transform the lives of both victims and perpetrators. Our goal is to create a supportive environment where rehabilitation is possible, breaking the cycle of violence for future generations. Together, we strive for a world where compassion, understanding, and safety are at the forefront of our interactions, ensuring that every person can thrive without fear.

SISSIES Approach

At Sissies House, we embrace a holistic framework known as the SISSIES Approach, which encompasses:

  • Safety: Creating a secure and welcoming environment essential for healing.
  • Inner Work: Encouraging self-reflection and personal growth to foster resilience.
  • Solidarity: Building a supportive community that uplifts and empowers individuals.
  • Spiritual Cleanse: Promoting mental and emotional wellness through mindfulness and self-care practices.
  • Inventing Yourself Again: Guiding individuals on a journey of rediscovery to reclaim their identities.
  • Eating Clean: Emphasizing the importance of nutrition as a vital component of recovery.
  • Surpassing Your Dreams: Empowering individuals to set and achieve their personal goals.

Through these principles, we aim to transform lives and create pathways to lasting change.
