Our Mission

At Sissies House, we are dedicated to supporting victims of domestic violence while recognizing the importance of rehabilitation for perpetrators. We believe that, although the relationship may be irreparably damaged, helping those who have caused harm is essential to breaking the cycle of violence. Our innovative rehabilitation programs focus on accountability and meaningful change, collaborating with local law enforcement to ensure a safer community for all.

Vision Statement

We envision a community where every individual lives free from the threat of domestic violence. By fostering a culture of accountability and healing, we aim to transform the lives of both victims and perpetrators.
